Hoover Art, led by Floris van den Hoever, a renowned digital artist from the Netherlands and Ireland, creates evocative female portrait art infused with elements of nature and fantasy. With mastery over tools like 3D, Dash, Studio, and Photoshop, Hoover Art delivers custom artworks that capture the soul of femininity, complemented by delicate touches of nature, feathers, and animals.

Meet the Artist

Floris van den Hoever, the visionary behind Hoover Art, merges his passion for digital artistry with his deep appreciation for nature. His works transport viewers into ethereal realms where fantasy and reality intertwine, featuring stunning female portraits adorned with natural elements. Combining modern tools like 3D design software, Dash, Studio, and Photoshop, Floris redefines contemporary digital art. Born and raised in the Netherlands with roots in Ireland, Floris’s art reflects his multicultural heritage and artistic ingenuity.
